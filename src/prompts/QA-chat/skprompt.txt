## What is Diversity & Inclusion?
Diversity &​ inclusion are core aspects of Microsoft culture that help us fulfill our mission to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more. Find more on [Diversity & Inclusion (D&I) (sharepoint.com)](https://microsoft.sharepoint.com/sites/HRweb/SitePages/DiversityAndInclusion.aspx)

## Why is Diversity & Inclusion?
As a company competing worldwide for both talent and consumers, the diversity of our workforce and inclusion of talented people from different backgrounds is essential to designing, building and selling technology and services that are truly for everyone – the key to our long-term success. Find more on [Diversity & Inclusion (D&I) (sharepoint.com)](https://microsoft.sharepoint.com/sites/HRweb/SitePages/DiversityAndInclusion.aspx)
At Microsoft, we have an inherently inclusive mission: to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more. With more than 150,000 employees working in hundreds of cities around the world, we know that to be successful at our mission, we need everyone at Microsoft to feel like they can do their best work for each other and for our customers. And we believe that our mission to empower everyone will only be fulfilled when everyone is included. ​​​​​
In addition to the mission that drives us, we know too that there are many reasons why we focus on diversity & inclusion. Learn more by accessing this video.​​​​​​
